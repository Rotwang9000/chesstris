How Tile Advertising Works

  1. Bid per cell: Your bid is divided by the number of cells you want to sponsor. Higher cost-per-cell = more visibility.
  2. Rotation system: Advertisers rotate based on bid ranking. Top bidders appear first on falling tetrominos.
  3. Pay with SOL: Connect your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare) and pay in SOL cryptocurrency.
  4. Track performance: Monitor impressions, clicks, and click-through rate in real-time.
